LUCIFER'S COLD EMBRACE

BIOGRAPHY

Handling back-up vocals and half the guitar- and songwriting
in Dutch band Non-Divine since he was seventeen,
Martin has a part of the international metal scene
for well over a decade. Non-Divine has supported bands
like Dio, Volbeat, Anthrax, Nevermore and After Forever,
played on many festivals, and toured Europe
with Flotsam & Jetsam and Izegrim.
Its debut album was was rewarded CD of the month
by the prestigious Aardschok magazine.
With Non-Divine on a break, Martin set his mind on treading new grounds.

Sharing his girlfriend's deep love of bands like Naglfar and Dissection,
he set out to write some songs for her. Being a perfectionist,
the dark project turned into something massive.
Dubbing this new spawn Lucifer's Cold Embrace,
he took it upon himself to write all music and lyrics;
perform, produce, mix and master the five brand new songs,
and unleash them world-wide...

...And he did.

Brutalizing double bass, grinding vocals, apocalyptic strings
and choirs fuse with Martin's keen sense of
melody and song-writing to form
a remarkable experience.
Ferociously brutal while hauntingly beautiful,
Sovereign Heresy will dig itself
into the listener's skull like an icicle.

Lucifer's Cold Embrace is here to stay.
